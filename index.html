Meet VoxLand

The FOSS, Extensible Multiplayer Voxel Engine

The Voxel Engine That You'll Love.

VoxLand is a FOSS, Extensible Multiplayer Voxel Engine. It is built on top of the Noa game engine.

Fully Open Source!

The VoxLand is Free and Open source. Everyone can help the project grow!
